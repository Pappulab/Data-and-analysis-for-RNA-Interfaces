loc_list_sorted: traced localization data
each columns:
1 - frame; 2-3 - x/y positions; 4 - brightness; 5 - molecule index; 6 - accumulated trajectory length;
7 - relative interfacial angle (decided by x/y positions and fitted circular interface)
8 - displacement distance; 9 - displacement angle; 10 - parallel displacement; 11 - perpendicular disaplacement.

jump_list: distances of all directional displacements
jump_angle: angles of all directional displacements
jump_par_list: distances of parallel displacements
jump_perp_list: distance of perpendicular displacements

pd_perp: perpendicular displacement Gaussian fit
pd_par: parallel displacement Gaussian fit
